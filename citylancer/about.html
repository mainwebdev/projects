#marketplace

CityLancer marketplace was setup to address the inefficient candidate acquisition process currently prevailing in the industry

#efficiency

Recruiters chase down candidates on industrial scale: phone calls, emails, job boards, social networks and recruitment events just for the chance of an introduction

#team

On CityLancer marketplace you engage top candidates into effectively becoming a part of your recruitment team - you work together towards a mutual goal - and share the reward in case of a job placement.

#rewards

Candidates want to get straight to the business: What commission, as a candidate, I will get in case of placement from this recruiter?

#engagement

This type of active engagement will make it possible to fill positions faster and with higher quality candidates.
