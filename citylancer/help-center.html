help center

Who is a Lancer?

A Lancer is a modern day professional office worker armed with skills and knowledge, presently employed in the industry

Why should I join?


1) Because you are pivotal, the most active yet unrewarded part of the recruitment process! You worked hard to reach this stage of your career, you sweat over your CV, you brushed up your skills and attended the interviews suited and booted. And at the end the person who recommended you or recruiter who placed you gets the reward -- Not you! We think this is not fair - Lancer is a very active part of the process and deserves a share of recruitment bonus.

2) Because you will get a real time anonymous appraisal of your skills by those in the real world marketplace.

3) Because you should capture more of the financial rewards from your next job placement.

Is it for me?

CityLancer is a marketplace for professional office workers: the real workhorses of the corporate world. It is not a marketplace for freelancers. You should have experience in the industry and be currently employed in your profession in order to qualify for an invitation.

How do I get an invite to CityLancer?

Please follow the link and request an invite to CityLancer. We shall process your request within 48 hours and further inform you by email

What is my profile?

Your profile on CityLancer consists of a concise version of your skills, preferred location, job history and expected remuneration.

Will recruiters see my personal contact details?

No, but recruiters will see your contact information after you review and accept the preliminary offer.

Is it free for Lancers?

Yes, this service is not only free but also guarantees financial rewards to every successfully placed Lancer.

How big a financial reward can I expect?

You will get the best negotiated market salary offers for your next job. In addition, the recruiter will offer a guaranteed minimum Referral Fee in the range of $250-$5000.
If you have more than five years of experience in the workplace, you can opt in for Split Fee (10-45% of total recruiter commission)

Who will pay the bonus?

The recruiter who places you or the company that hires you in case no external recruiter is involved. Depending on the job offer terms, you will agree to a Referral OR Split fee type of agreement with the recruiter even before you disclose your identity. You and the recruiter enter into Signup bonus Agreements that come into force only in case of a successful placement.

Can I trust every offer I get on CityLancer?

No. Unfortunately some recruiters try to abuse and game the system to entice candidates. At CityLancer we identify and block such accounts as soon as possible. However, you should treat job offers on CityLancer with the same degree of caution as you would those from any other source.

What is CityLancer marketplace?

The CityLancer marketplace is a platform where recruiters can invest their candidate acquisition budget by offering part of that money directly to the Lancer as an incentive package. The message for the Lancer is: Work with me on this job (if you like the role) and get a part of my fee if I place you

Why should I join?


To save money. And time. CityLancer is the marketplace where where you INVITE active and financially motivated candidates to work on your jobs.

Our pool of Candidates is comprised of currently employed office professionals who are ready to change career subject to motivating offer. At CityLancer marketplace we let you introduce yourself and propose to this pool.

CityLancer is free of charge at the moment (Please see the How it Works page)

You have ZERO upfront costs - you only pay a predetermined bonus in CASE OF a successful placement.

How to access the CityLancer marketplace?


To make the Recruiter experience more successful and profitable, we limit the number of competing agencies in any given geographical area. We will review every recruiter application as part of our standard verification process.

There are no restrictions for hiring company HR.

Please follow this link to request your invite.

Exactly how much will CityLancer cost me?

Currently, CityLancer is totally free of charge for Recruiters. Your monthly invoice will be automatically marked as paid by us. For the future, we have implemented a Fair Recruiter Incentive Credit System in which Recruiters receive a FREE monthly credit worth $200 and a further $200 for each successful Placement. So you can practically use CityLancer Free of Charge.
Please remember, your main cost of Lancer acquisition is the Signup Bonus amount you agree to pay in case of placement. See an example here

Exactly how can I save money?

Let’s assume your candidate acquisition budget is $X’000. On CityLancer you will always reward your successfully placed candidate with an amount less than $X’000. The CityLancer difference is what you save.

Exactly how can I save time?


On CityLancer it takes a few minutes to setup your recruiter profile, list jobs, and invite lancers. The CityLancer System will automatically update you with matching Lancers, their CVs and counteroffers.

Consider how much time you spend on job boards and social networks, reviewing endless stream of irrelevant CVs and chasing “passive job seekers” on the phone. CityLancer lets you achieve the same in just a few minutes!

The CityLancer difference is what you save in terms of valuable time.

Is it always necessary to offer a Signup bonus to Lancers?

Rewarding Lancers is not a mandatory option on CityLancer. A Lancer can also be attracted with a higher salary package, a blue chip company, a challenging job or other exciting benefits.
Lancers will evaluate your offer against offers from other recruiters. The Signup bonus is a sure way to promote yourself and engage candidates into an active recruitment process.

Do you favor Recruitment Agencies or Internal HR Departments?

Professional Recruitment Agencies and Inhouse HR teams are equally welcome on CityLancer. This is an open marketplace where you present your offers and bonuses. It is up to the Lancers to decide which offers they find the most appealing.

What are the main guidelines to follow on CityLancer as a Recruiter?

It's all about healthy relations between you and your pool of professional City Lancers. Don’t treat this marketplace as a CV database. Every Lancer is experienced and employed in the industry - treat them as your partners for a successful collaboration towards a placement

Why I must always reveal the offered salary and bonus in advance? Will my bargaining position suffer?

In order not to lose yours and the candidates’ time and effort, Lancers set certain expectations for the next job, particularly the remuneration package. We need to guide you to those Lancers who are in your compensation bracket and hence set the tone for further negotiation.

Do I enter into a binding agreement with Lancers on the CityLancer marketplace?

Yes you do. But only in case of a successful placement you are required to make a payment to the successfully placed Lancer.
Everything that takes place after the successful introduction at CityLancer is handled by you entirely. CityLancer does not get involved in the recruitment process beyond the invitation of Lancers to a job. You continue from here on your own.

Can I see an example of a ReferralFee and/or SplitFee Agreement?

Example: ReferralFee.pdf, SplitFee.pdf

How do you verify Lancers who enter the marketplace?

All our Lancers are currently part of the active workforce. We cannot guarantee that Lancers will not attempt to provide false information about themselves but we have created an environment to discourage and prevent such behaviour.
