Welcome Bonus

This is the default fee Recruiters pay Lancers upon successful placement. This fee varies from $0 to $5000. The higher the figure, the higher the chance of engaging Lancers into interviews stage. If your job offer is above the market rate,- Lancers are likely to consider this job even without a bonus

Referral Auction

If you haven’t found the Lancer right away, create a Search Alert for a given skill set, geographical location and salary range. As soon as Lancers with these in-demand skills enter the market we alert you. The Bid amount for each individual Search Alert dictates your place in the queue among competing Recruiters

Split Fee

Motivate senior-level Lancers by sharing 5% to 45% of your commission instead of splitting it with competing Recruiters. The exact percentage will be dictated by market conditions, particularly the supply and demand for specific skills and Lancers. If you’re an internal HR, you can provide a higher salary package
