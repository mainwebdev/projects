How to Recognize Prostatitis or Other Disorders of the Genitourinary System in Men?

Remember the ironic poem that ends with the lines "In the apartment, conversations have ceased, the night light is barely burning. It's dark... All the curtains are down... The husband is thirty-seven and two"?

Women know that for every representative of the stronger sex, even a minor health problem is a real catastrophe. And now, imagine how any disorders of the male reproductive system hit their self-esteem and confidence! And this is not pretense – men truly feel vulnerable, depressed, and helpless when facing prostatitis or other diseases of the genitourinary system. It becomes awkward to go to the doctor, voice their problems... A man closes in on himself, searches for solutions on the internet, and may worsen the problem. Therefore, it's important not to overlook the symptoms and discuss the need to consult with a doctor.

So, what can indicate diseases of the genitourinary system?

  • • Pain, burning, and tingling in the groin, scrotum, shooting pains in the groin, lumbar, and sacral regions
  • • Discomfort during urination
  • • Frequent trips to the toilet (more than once every 2 hours)
  • • Pain during erection, sexual intercourse, and orgasm (he may complain of discomfort)
  • • Decreased potency (a man may, without mentioning it, refuse intimacy, explaining it by headaches, lack of mood, fatigue, etc.)
  • • Blood impurities, odor in urine and sperm

And the emotional state described above.

Why is it so important to pay attention to this? Because without medical assistance, complications can include bladder stones, kidney failure, infertility, urinary incontinence, sexual disorders, infection, chronic pain, and much more.

So, take care of yourself and your men!
