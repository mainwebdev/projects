How to Recognize Prostatitis or Other Disorders of the Genitourinary System in Men?

Remember the ironic poem ending with the lines "In the apartment, conversations have ceased, the nightlight is barely lit. It's dark... All the curtains are down... The husband is thirty-seven and two"?

Women know that for every representative of the strong gender, even a minor health problem is a real catastrophe. And now, imagine how any disorders in the male reproductive system hit his self-esteem and confidence! And it's not posing – men really feel vulnerable, depressed, and helpless when faced with prostatitis or other genitourinary diseases. It becomes awkward to go to the doctor, to voice their problems... Men close up, seek solutions on the internet, and may worsen the problem. Therefore, it is essential not to overlook symptoms and talk about the need to consult a doctor.

So, what can indicate disorders of the genitourinary system?

  • • Pain, burning, and tingling in the perineum, scrotum, shooting pains in the groin, lower back, and sacrum
  • • Discomfort during urination
  • • Frequent trips to the toilet (more than once every 2 hours)
  • • Pain during erection, sexual intercourse, and orgasm (he may complain of discomfort)
  • • Reduced potency (a man may refuse intimacy without talking about it, explaining it by headaches, lack of mood, fatigue, etc.)
  • • Blood impurities, odor in urine and semen

And the described emotional state above.

Why is it so important to pay attention to this? Because without medical assistance, complications can include bladder stones, kidney failure, infertility, urinary incontinence, sexual disorders, infection, chronic pain, and much more.

So, take care of yourself and your men!
