How to Recognize That a Man Has Prostatitis or Other Disorders of the Genitourinary System?

Remember the ironic poem ending with the lines "In the apartment, conversations have ceased, the nightlight barely glows. It's dark... All the curtains are lowered... The husband is thirty-seven and two"?

Women know that for every representative of the strong sex, even a minor health problem is a real disaster. And now imagine how any disorders in the male reproductive system hit their self-esteem and confidence! And this is not posing – men really feel vulnerable, oppressed, helpless when faced with prostatitis or other diseases of the genitourinary system. It becomes awkward to go to the doctor, to voice their problems... A man closes in on himself, seeks solutions on the internet, and can exacerbate the problem. Therefore, it is important not to overlook the symptoms and talk about the need to consult a doctor.

So, what can indicate diseases of the genitourinary system?

  • • Pain, burning, and tingling in the groin, scrotum, shooting pains in the groin, lower back, and sacrum
  • • Discomfort during urination
  • • Frequent visits to the toilet (more than once every 2 hours)
  • • Pain during erection, sexual intercourse, and orgasm (he may complain of discomfort)
  • • Reduced potency (a man may refuse intimacy without mentioning it, explaining it with a headache, lack of mood, fatigue, etc.)
  • • Blood in the urine and semen, odor in urine and semen

And the emotional state described above.

Why is it so important to pay attention to this? Because without medical help, complications can include bladder stones, kidney failure, infertility, urinary incontinence, sexual disorders, infection, chronic pain, and much more.

So, take care of yourself and your men!
