How to recognize that a man has prostatitis or other disorders of the genitourinary system?

Remember the ironic poem ending with the lines "In the apartment, conversations have ceased, the night light barely glows. It's dark… All the curtains are lowered… The man is thirty-seven and two"?

Women know that for every representative of the stronger sex, even a minor health problem is a real catastrophe. And now imagine how disorders of the male reproductive system hit their self-esteem and confidence! And this is not posing – men really feel vulnerable, oppressed, helpless when faced with prostatitis or other genitourinary diseases. It becomes awkward to go to the doctor, voice their problems... A man closes up, looks for solutions on the internet, and may worsen the problem. So, it is important not to overlook the symptoms and talk about the need to consult a doctor.

So, what can indicate diseases of the genitourinary system?

  • • pain, burning, and tingling in the groin, scrotum, shooting pains in the groin, lower back, and sacrum
  • • discomfort during urination
  • • frequent trips to the toilet (more than once every 2 hours)
  • • pain during erection, sexual intercourse, and orgasm (he may complain of discomfort)
  • • decreased potency (a man may refuse intimacy, explaining it with a headache, lack of mood, fatigue, etc.)
  • • blood in urine and semen, and an unusual odor

And the described emotional state above.

Why is it so important to pay attention to this? Because without medical assistance, complications can include bladder stones, kidney failure, infertility, urinary incontinence, sexual disorders, infection, chronic pain, and much more.

So take care of yourself and your men!
