INSTRUCTIONS

Hello, you've entered BrainDashboard – the brain control panel. This system will help you understand the basis of your abilities. It connects behavioral skills with the functioning of specific brain functions. BrainDashboard allows you to assess and develop the necessary qualities of your intelligence and behavior.

Working with BrainDashboard is very simple:

  • Choose the skill you want to explore in yourself. To do this, click on the desired skill in the list on the right side of the screen; it will be highlighted.
  • Study the behavioral manifestations (indicators) that define this skill. To do this, navigate through the layers and read the descriptions of the behavioral skill under the footnote on the left side of the screen. Each layer corresponds to a separate indicator.
  • Study the brain functions on which the skill is based. To do this, press the red button COGNITIVE FUNCTIONS AND SKILLS at the bottom left of the screen.
  • Take the diagnostic system to accurately assess the selected skill. To do this, click the red ANALYZE button at the bottom left on the Cognitive Functions and Skills screen.
