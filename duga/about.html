STUDIO

Know more about our studio life

We have not only got serious and long-term education, but also have achieved extensive experience in residential and commercial interior design and realization both in Russia and outside.

We are united by a genuine passion and full devotion to our profession.

In our practice we use all the advantages of team work. We find ourselves in constant search of efficiency enhancement methods. We like experimenting and looking for new approaches to everyday task solution.

We can call “healthy” perfectionism one of our biggest advantages, together with broad geography of our projects and invaluable experience.

We don’t try to find an easier and faster way of achieving success in our work. Our results, no matter how good they are, will not ever leave us completely satisfied. This, as well as our knowledge and gathered experience, helps us in our permanent eagerness to go ahead.

We look upon our ideal, first of all, in the context of rationality and effectiveness. However, our experience has proven that the best solution is at the same time integral to aesthetic perfection.

Though our first projects will soon turn 10 years old, we are still full of enthusiasm which is supported not only with our experience, but mainly with our ability to work as a single organism with the only aim of existence being the achievement of the best result.
